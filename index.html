Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information Collection

MyVacations does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal information.

2. Local Storage

All trips, expenses, budgets and travel information are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to external servers.

3. Internet Connection

The application works offline. An Internet connection is only required to download or update the application through Google Play.

4. Third-party Services

MyVacations does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs or any tracking technologies.

5. Data Sharing

No user information is shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in future versions of the application.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the Google Play listing.
